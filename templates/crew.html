About the Crew

Please excuse the third person voice.

Carolyn and David met in late 2004 on a blind date and took their first sailing class together in April 2005 at Harbor Sailboats in San Diego. They learned to sail on Capri 22's in San Diego Bay. Neither of them had any sailing experience, but after their first weekend long course both of them were hooked.
