About the Boat

Crazy Love is a Canadian built 1984 JJ Taylor 26, better known as a Contessa 26. Carolyn and I bought her in October 2011 in Dana Point, CA for about $12,000.

More information about the JJ Taylor/Contessa 26 can be found at bluewaterboats.org
